Vaccines

Read

Vaccination is beneficial from an individual point of view. Vaccination is the process of protecting a person from disease by stimulating their immune system. Preventive vaccines prevent infectious diseases. Therapeutic vaccines help patients fight an existing disease, such as cancer.

Vaccine research objectives:

  • produce new vaccines;;
  • improve the comfort, tolerance and effectiveness of existing vaccines.
